# Privacy Policy — Simple Annotations

**Effective date:** February 26, 2026

## Overview

Simple Annotations is a Figma plugin that lets you attach structured annotation notes to elements on the Figma canvas. This policy explains how the plugin handles your data.

## Data We Collect

Simple Annotations does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Stored Locally

The plugin stores the following data **locally on your device** using Figma's built-in `clientStorage` API:

- **Annotation labels (tags)** — the short label names and colors you create or reuse across annotations

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the plugin developer or any third party. It can be cleared at any time by Figma.

Annotation content (labels, descriptions, connector settings, theme preferences) is also saved as **plugin data on the Figma canvas** within your Figma file, which is governed by [Figma's own Privacy Policy](https://www.figma.com/privacy/).

## Network Requests

Simple Annotations makes **no network requests**. It operates entirely within the Figma plugin sandbox with no external connections.

## Third-Party Services

The plugin does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data services.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above will be updated and the change will be noted in the plugin's release notes.

## Contact

For questions about this policy or the plugin, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/mathewsamueldesign/figma-simple-annotations/issues).
